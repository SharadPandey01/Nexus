# Master Prompt Test Event 1: The AI Horizons Summit 2026

**Event Description:**
"Create a 3-day flagship tech conference called 'AI Horizons Summit 2026' taking place from October 15th to October 17th at the Moscone Center in San Francisco. We expect 5000 attendees, including developers, researchers, and enterprise AI leaders. The core themes are 'Autonomous Agents in Production', 'Ethical AI Frameworks', and 'The Future of Compute'. Key activities include a CEO keynote on day 1, hands-on agent building workshops on day 2, and an investor pitch competition on day 3. The organizer is 'Nexus Global Tech'. We have a primary venue 'Moscone West' and a secondary venue 'Hotel W for VIP dinners'."

**Follow-up Prompts to Test System Behavior:**

**1. Testing Chronos (Scheduling & Optimization):**
"Hey Chronos, build out the schedule for Day 1. It needs to start with registration at 8 AM, the CEO Keynote at 9:30 AM in Moscone West Main Hall, and two parallel tracks at 11 AM (one for 'Agents' and one for 'Compute'). After lunch at 12:30 PM, we need an afternoon panel on 'Ethical AI', and wrap up with a networking mixer at 5 PM."

**2. Testing Apollo (Content & Campaign):**
"Apollo, please create a promotional campaign for the Day 2 'hands-on agent building workshops'. Target developers who want to learn practical skills. We need this pushed on LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram. Adopt a highly technical but exciting tone. Also, make sure to suggest an image prompt for the social posts."

**3. Testing Hermes (Mail & Comms):**
"Hermes, draft an email to the VIP attendees inviting them to the exclusive dinner at Hotel W on Day 1. Ensure the tone is highly professional and welcoming. Use placeholders for their names and organizations. Also, segment our current participant list to find people whose role is 'VIP' or 'Speaker'."

**4. Testing Fortuna (Finance & Budget):**
"Fortuna, we have a total budget of $500,000 for this event. We have already spent $150,000 on venue booking (Moscone Center) and $50,000 on catering. We are considering hiring a celebrity AI speaker for $75,000. Analyze our current budget, calculate the remaining funds, and advise if the speaker fee is financially sound or if we should negotiate."

**5. Testing Athena (Analytics & Insights):**
"Athena, based on previous tech summits, our goal is to achieve an 85% attendee satisfaction rate and a 40% VIP attendance rate. Can you outline the key performance indicators (KPIs) we should track during the summit, and suggest what data we need to collect at registration and after the workshops to measure this?"

**6. Testing Cross-Agent Cascading (Complex Request):**
"We need to move the CEO keynote from 9:30 AM to 10:30 AM because his flight is delayed. Chronos, update the schedule. Then, tell Hermes to draft an urgent apology email to all attendees about the delay, and tell Apollo to put out a quick Twitter update."
